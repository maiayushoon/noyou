Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Not legal advice. This document is provided for transparency and general information only. It is not legal advice and does not create an attorney–client relationship. Consult a qualified lawyer in your jurisdiction before relying on it for compliance purposes.

1. Who we are

NoYou ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered digital-reputation service that helps individuals discover, understand, and manage how they are represented across the public web. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how long we keep it, and the rights you have over it. It applies to the NoYou web application and API.

2. Data we collect

We collect only what we need to provide the service:

  • Account data — your email address, optional full name, and a securely hashed password. We never store your password in plain text.
  • Monitored identities — the handles, profile names, or search terms you ask us to track (for example a Twitter/X handle or a person's name), and the platforms they belong to.
  • Public mentions — publicly available content we discover that references your monitored identities: the source, URL, author, title, text excerpt, and the date it was published or discovered.
  • Derived analysis — AI-generated metadata about each mention, such as sentiment, a risk level and category, and a short reasoning summary, plus your overall reputation score.
  • Operational data — records of scans you run, alerts we raise, and cleanup actions you take, so the product can show history and trends.

We do not intentionally collect special-category data (such as health, religion, or political opinions). Public mentions are gathered from third-party sources and may incidentally contain such information; you can delete any mention or your entire account at any time (see Section 6).

3. How we use your data

  • To run scans across configured sources and surface mentions of you.
  • To analyze and score that content (sentiment, risk, recommendations) so you can prioritize what matters.
  • To notify you of high-risk content or meaningful changes to your score.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the service.

Our legal bases (where GDPR applies) are performance of a contract (providing the service you signed up for), our legitimate interests in operating and securing the product, and your consent where required. We do not sell your personal information.

4. Third-party sources and processors

To find public mentions, NoYou may query third-party search and platform APIs (for example general web search, Twitter/X, Reddit, or YouTube). When you connect such sources, your queries and the public results are processed accordingly. AI analysis may be performed by a configured model provider. These third parties act as independent controllers or as our processors under their own terms; we share only what is necessary to perform a scan or an analysis.

5. Cookies and local storage

NoYou does not use advertising or tracking cookies. After you sign in, your authentication token (a JWT) is stored in your browser's localStorage so you stay logged in between visits. Clearing your browser storage or signing out removes it. This token is strictly necessary for the service to function.

6. Data retention

We keep your account and associated data for as long as your account is active. Scan results, mentions, and analysis are retained so we can show history and trends until you delete them or close your account. When you delete your account, all associated records are permanently removed (see Section 7). We may retain minimal records where required to comply with legal obligations.

7. Your rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you have rights over your personal data, including the right to access, correct, export ("portability"), and delete it ("right to be forgotten" / "right to delete"), and to object to or restrict certain processing. CCPA additionally gives California residents the right to know what we collect and to opt out of any sale of personal information — and we do not sell it.

NoYou makes two of these rights available directly in the product:

  • Export your data — an authenticated request to GET /api/v1/privacy/export returns a single JSON file containing your profile, monitored accounts, mentions and their analysis, alerts, cleanup actions, and scan history.
  • Delete your account — an authenticated request to DELETE /api/v1/privacy/account permanently and irreversibly deletes your account and every associated record.

To exercise any other right, or if you have questions, contact us at support@noyou.app. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

8. Security

We protect your data with industry-standard measures: passwords are hashed with bcrypt, access is authenticated with signed tokens, and every data request is scoped to the logged-in user so you can only ever access your own records. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to keep your information safe.

9. Children

NoYou is not intended for anyone under 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the service evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, communicated to you. Continued use of NoYou after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

Questions about privacy or your data? Email support@noyou.app.
